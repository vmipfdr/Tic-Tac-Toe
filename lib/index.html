Matt's Tic Tac Toe

I threw together a simple game of Tic Tac Toe that has HTML, CSS & Javascript.

The game is played on a grid that's 3 squares by 3 squares. Player One (odd clicks) is Blue, Player Two (even clicks) is is Red. The first player to get 3 of their marks in a row (up, down, across, or diagonally) is the winner. When all 9 squares are full, the game is over.
